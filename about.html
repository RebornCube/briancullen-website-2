About Me

Hi, I'm Brian Cullen — a GIS Analyst, MSc student in Geographic Information Science, and someone who loves turning complex spatial data into meaningful, accessible solutions. I work across GIS automation, field data collection, network analysis, and web-based mapping, with a strong focus on building elegant, reliable workflows.

I'm currently based in Ireland, working in the Environmental Planning & Renewables (EPR) sector of RPS where I support high-impact geospatial projects.

What I Do

With a solid foundation in GIS and programming, I specialize in creating efficient geospatial solutions that streamline workflows and enhance data visualization. My expertise includes:

  • GIS Analysis & Mapping
  • Python Scripting & Automation
  • Web Mapping & Application Development
  • Spatial Data Management

My Interests
